Problem

What's wrong with falling?

Number One Cause of Injury Deaths

In seniors, falls are the number one cause of fractures, hospital admissions for trauma, loss of independence, and injury deaths.

Signs of Other Medical Issues

Falls can be a sign of a new medical problem that needs attention, such as diabetes or changes in blood pressure. Falls can also be a sign of problems with medications or eyesight that can be easily corrected once detected.

Solution

What makes Vital Patch the bees' knees.
